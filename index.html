ABOUT HAALO - HOME SALON

FIRST OF ITS KIND IN COIMBATORE

Haalo is a startup in Coimbatore that offers a haircut at your doorstep with all safety measures. This service is more of a mobile barber shop wherein, you can make an appointment as per your convenience and then we will send you a professional barber without any hassle or the need to leave your house.

WHY HAALO WAS STARTED ?

In human civilization first preference is always given to maintain a hygenice body which includes hairdressing and spa services . After Covid-19 its a well known fact that the virus spreads fast throgh touching each other and mass exposure so we are in a situatiion where one has to be very careful in visiting salon or doing related activities .Thus We at Haalo have come up with Haircut at home which is an ultimate solution for the problem.
